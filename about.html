What is Grappling?

Grappling is a martial-art form, or a hand-to-hand combat technique. It does not rely on striking the opponent, but rather gripping them to either immobilize or injure them. Many martial arts use this technique. Some of the most well known is Wrestling, Judo, JiuJitsu and other non-striking martial arts.

What Do We Do?

The grappling club aims to teach students a variety of grappling moves from multiple martial art backgrounds. Everytime we meet we teach a new move and we spar. We mainly focus on JiuJitsu moves, however we also teach Judo and Wrestling moves to create an effective base.

Why You Should Join

Joining the grappling club would be beneficial to all students at SFS. This is because not only is grappling healthy for the body, it builds strength and endurance. In addition, it teaches discipline, and self-defence. The moves that are taught at the grappling club can be used in real-applications and can help defend someone if they need it. Joining the grappling club is a step towards a safer and healthier life.
