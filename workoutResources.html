Workout Resources

Physical fitness plays a large role in grappling. Strength assists with difficult moves and overpowring an opponent. While endurance allows you to last long matches without getting tired.

Strength Routines

Bodyweight Recommended Routine r/Fitness Beginner Routine nSuns LP Bodyweight Minimalist Routine
The Bodyweight Recommended routine is a routine which requires no equipment. It is aimed at beginners but can scale to intermediate people. It is effective, and it only runs 3 days a week.
The r/fitness beginner routine is a basic strength routine targeted for absolute beginners. It requires access to the gym and functions like "training wheels" on a bike. It focuses on developing stability in the large barbell lifts so that you can move on to a more advanced plan.
nSuns LP is a linear-progression based workout plan. It requires access to a gym and it focuses on the big lifts. These are Benchpress, Squat, Deadlift and Overhead Press
The Bodyweightfitness Minimalist Routine is a routine which requires no equipment. This routine is designed to be the "bare minimum" to stay healthy. This routine is if you are restricted on time, and just want to stay healthy, and feel the benefits of working out.

Endurance and Conditioning Training

Running Swimming High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) Agility Training
Running is one of the best ways to do conditioning training. Running everyday for a distance you set is great, however if you would like to train and structure your running read this running training guide.
Swimming is a full body workout and is excellent for conditioning. Swimming laps in a pool is an effective way to increase heartrate and condition the body. If you would like swim workouts, read this swim workout guide.
High Intensity Interval Training is a great way to increase heartrate, burn fat and increase cardiovascular endurance. It is very high in intensity and that is what makes it so effective. Here is a 8-week HIIT plan.
Agility is the ability to move quickly and easily. This mainly applies while standing during grappling, however it is important. Here are 25 agility drills for every athlete.
